Pregnancy Nutrition and Exercise Recommendation Model

This machine learning model is designed to recommend personalized food and exercise plans for pregnant mothers. The model uses various input features related to a mother's health and pregnancy stage, such as:

  • User ID: Unique identifier for each user.
  • Age: The age of the user.
  • Weight (kg) and Height (cm): Body measurements used to calculate BMI (Body Mass Index).
  • BMI: Body Mass Index, an indicator of body fat based on weight and height.
  • Trimester: Current pregnancy trimester (1st, 2nd, or 3rd), which influences nutritional and exercise needs.
  • Daily Calorie Intake: The amount of calories the user consumes daily.
  • Preferred Diet: The type of diet preferred by the user (e.g., vegetarian, vegan, balanced diet).
  • Allergies: Known food allergies that the model considers when recommending meals.
  • Exercise Level: The userâ€™s current level of physical activity.
  • Medical Conditions: Any medical conditions that need to be factored into the recommendations.

Using this data, the model generates personalized food and exercise plans that cater to the unique nutritional and physical activity requirements of pregnant women. It considers factors such as dietary restrictions, allergies, and health conditions to ensure the recommendations are safe and beneficial.

The model aims to promote optimal maternal health by aligning nutritional and exercise guidelines with the specific needs of each individual.
